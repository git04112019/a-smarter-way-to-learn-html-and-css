51 - Forms: textarea

Notes
    <textarea name="message" rows="4" cols="30"></textarea>
    * The textarea tag creates a multi-line text field.
    * Notice, first, that this tag is closed, with </textarea>.
    * rows="8" cols="30" specifies the number of visible rows and columns.
